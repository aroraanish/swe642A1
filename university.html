Welcome to Computer Science Department

The study of computing, data structures, and algorithms is known as computer science, and it is essential to modern society. Computer science is at the core of innovation in today's tech-driven culture, powering everything from smartphones to self- driving automobiles. Its importance is seen in domains such as data analysis, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence, where it is essential for resolving complex tasks. Within universities or colleges, computer science departments are academic establishments committed to the study, education, and research of computer science. George Mason University fosters an in- depth knowledge of computer science fundamentals through a wide range of academic programmes, from undergraduate to doctorate degrees. The advancement of the discipline through research in these departments results in innovations in software development, technology, and other areas.

During my academic projects, I had the opportunity to work on diverse data-driven projects that showcase my ability to handle complex data analysis tasks. For instance, in the AH Provisional COVID-19 Deaths Big Data Project, I collected and pre- processed data using Python and R, applied statistical analysis techniques, and developed data visualizations using libraries Like Matplotlib and ggplot. In addition to that I worked on the LoyaltyINC Sales Android Application Development project, I designed the database schema, implemented it using SQL and MySQL, and integrated it with the Android Studio application using Java for Database systems project. Adding to that, I have worked on another project for the MoldTelecom Website System Analysis and Design project, I created system models using UML. designed the user interface.
